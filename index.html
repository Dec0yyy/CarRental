FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About the Car Rental Booking Process on Our Website: Answers to Common Concerns and Inquiries.

1. Can I increase the mileage limit?
Yes, in this case, select the appropriate option when booking a car.
2. Are the cars insured?
Yes, we provide secondary insurance for all trips.
3. What if I need extra time?
When booking a car, you specify the duration of your rental. If you need some extra time, a special tariff will be applied.
3. How do I pay for the ride?
You pay only when your ride is over. Click on the relevant button in the app, and the cost of your ride will be calculated.
